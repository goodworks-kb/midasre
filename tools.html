Neighborhood & Schools Information

About Flushing, Queens

Flushing is a vibrant, diverse neighborhood in Queens, New York, known for its rich cultural heritage, excellent schools, and convenient access to Manhattan. Located in Queens Community District 7, Flushing offers a mix of residential and commercial properties with strong community values.

School Districts & Ratings

Flushing is served by New York City Department of Education (NYC DOE) School District 25. The area features excellent public and private schools.

School Resources:

  • NYC Department of Education - Official NYC schools website
  • GreatSchools.org - School ratings and reviews
  • NYC School Enrollment - Enrollment information
  • School Transportation - Bus and transportation info

School District 25 Highlights:

  • Serves Flushing, Whitestone, College Point, and parts of Bayside
  • Multiple elementary, middle, and high schools
  • Strong academic programs and extracurricular activities
  • Diverse student population with multilingual support

Local Amenities

Public Transportation:
  • 7 Train (Flushing-Main St station)
  • Multiple bus lines (Q12, Q13, Q15, Q16, Q17, Q20, Q25, Q26, Q27, Q28, Q44, Q48, Q58, Q65, Q66)
  • Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) - Flushing station
Parks & Recreation:
  • Flushing Meadows Corona Park - 897 acres
  • Kissena Park - 234 acres
  • Queens Botanical Garden
  • Multiple playgrounds and sports facilities
Shopping & Dining:
  • Flushing Mall and Main Street shopping district
  • Diverse restaurants (Chinese, Korean, Japanese, American)
  • Queens Crossing shopping center
  • Local markets and specialty stores
Healthcare:
  • NewYork-Presbyterian Queens Hospital
  • Flushing Hospital Medical Center
  • Multiple clinics and medical offices

Community Resources

  • Queens Public Library - Flushing Branch
  • Queens Community Board 7
  • NYC City Services
