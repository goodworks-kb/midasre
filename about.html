About Midas Realty

About Tina Kim

Tina Kim began her real estate career in 1986 and has built a long-standing reputation for exceptional service and successful real estate transactions.

With over 38 years of experience, Tina is known for her dedication, strong work ethic, and ability to understand and meet the specific needs of her clients. Her commitment to excellence has earned Midas Realty a steady stream of referrals and returning clients.

Dedicated to creating thriving neighborhoods, Tina has been actively involved in community leadership throughout her career. She served as President of the Korean American Realtor Association (K.A.R.A.) from 2010 to 2011, organizing real estate education programs, seminars, and community events that strengthened connections among realtors, buyers, and sellers.

Tina is committed to integrity, professionalism, and personalized service. She is fluent in Korean and English and works with clients of all backgrounds. When needed, translation tools are used to ensure clear communication and a positive experience for everyone, regardless of language.
